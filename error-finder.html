DZ Error Finder

Follow these 3 steps to find the exact errors:

Step 1: Go to the DZ Editor tab

Make sure you're on deadzone.dev/editor/597

Step 2: Open Console

Press F12 on your keyboard, then click the Console tab at the top

If it says "type allow pasting" - type: allow pasting and press Enter

Step 3: Click this button, then paste in Console

After copying, click in the Console area, press Ctrl+V, then Enter

An alert box will pop up showing ALL errors with line numbers!

Screenshot the alert and send it to Brad!

The script (for reference):

alert(monaco.editor.getModelMarkers({}).map(function(m){return 'L'+m.startLineNumber+': '+m.message}).join('\n'))
